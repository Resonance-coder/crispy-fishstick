Standardized Course Numbering System

Many educational institutions and training programs use a four-digits numbering system for consistency.
This can help learners and instructors quickly identify the course level and category.

Each Module number follows this pattern:

  • 1000 Series: Basic Training Modules
  • 2000 Series: Intensive Training Modules
  • 2100 Series: Career-Specific Modules (Part of Intensive Training Modules)
  • 2200 Series: Hospitality Training Modules (Supported by PBG / One of Intensive Trainings)
